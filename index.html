2. The Geometric Scales

The code iterates through integer nodes $k \in \mathbb{Z}^+$ across four fundamental scales defined in the GeometricScales class.

Lepton Scale (N)

Based on the logarithmic geometry of spacetime.

$$ E_L(k) = k \cdot m_e \cdot (4\pi N^3) $$

Base Energy ($k=1$): ~104.12 MeV
Matches: Muon ($k=1$), Tau ($k=17$)
Meson Scale (\(\alpha^{-1}\))

Based on the electromagnetic coupling strength.

$$ E_M(k) = k \cdot m_e \cdot \alpha^{-1} $$

Base Energy ($k=1$): ~70.02 MeV
Matches: Pion ($k=2$), Kaon ($k=7$)
Baryon Scale (\(\pi^5\))

Based on 10D hyperspherical geometry.

$$ E_B(k) = k \cdot m_e \cdot \pi^5 $$

Base Energy ($k=1$): ~156.37 MeV
Matches: Proton ($k=6$), Lambda ($k=7$)
Boson Scale (\(\alpha^{-2}\))

High-energy lattice for weak/Higgs interactions.

$$ E_W(k) = k \cdot m_e \cdot (\pi \alpha^{-2}) $$

Base Energy ($k=1$): ~30,137 MeV
Matches: Z Boson, Higgs ($k \approx 4$)
